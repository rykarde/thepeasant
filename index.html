About

Game Development

The project is the development of the game The Adventure of The Peasant, so the target audience of my work are players from 7 to 18 years. In this project I realized the environmental design, the design of the user interface, called UI, the advertising material of the game, its website and merchandising.

Design

The design chosen was low poly to make the game lighter for less powerful computers and because I found it very captivating for the target audience who is 7-18 years old.

Marketing

For the markting campain I made posters, invites for the release and merchandising of the game
